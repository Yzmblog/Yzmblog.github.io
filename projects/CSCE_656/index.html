Section 3 — A Challenge I Face With Media Tools

One difficulty I often run into is managing files and formats across different apps/devices. For example, photos and videos can look different depending on the platform (phone vs. laptop, different players, or different export settings), and that makes it harder to keep the “same” content consistent when sharing or editing. Another related challenge is keeping storage organized—media files are large, and it’s easy for versions to pile up.

  • Hardware limitation: limited storage / battery life on mobile devices
  • Software issue: inconsistent exports (resolution, color, compression)
  • Workflow pain: too many apps; hard to keep a clean, repeatable process
